Children under the age of 12 are only permitted to visit the museum if accompanied by an adult. Unsupervised children aged 12 and over can enter the museum, but they may not supervise younger children.

Route beschrijving

NEMO is easily accessible by public transport. The museum's eye-catching copper-green building is easy to spot from Amsterdam Central Station. You can also reach the museum by bike or car. From Central Station On foot: A 15-minute walk. Leave the main entrance (cityside) of Central Station and turn left.
By bus: Take Bus 22 and get off at Kadijksplein. From the bus stop it is a 5-minute walk. You can plan your journey at 9292.nl or gvb.nl

There is currently construction on the road around the Central Station. Due to diversions, the duration is sometimes slightly longer. Navigating via Google maps is not always reliable due to many changes.
Tip: Use ANWB routeplanner.

By car NEMO is accessible by car, however parking in the centre of Amsterdam is expensive.

Address for navigation systems: Oosterdok 2, 1011 VX Amsterdam.
